## Significant Discovery Alert

### 1. API/UI split is a process failure, not a surprise

Epic 2 revealed that **"frontend out of scope" without a linked UI story** allows epics to reach "done" while evaluators cannot use delivered capabilities. Story 2.8 fixed the product; **process must prevent recurrence**.

### 2. Epic completion ≠ story count

Running a retro at 8/8 stories while `/credentials` was still a placeholder produced accurate backend lessons but **premature epic closure signal**. Epic completion must include product-surface verification.

## Team Agreements

1. **"Done" means story file, sprint-status, and review gate align** — not sprint-status alone.
2. **Backend-only is not silent** — API surface without UI requires a linked story or honest placeholder; never implied deferral.
3. **Security CI guards ship in `make ci` the same story** that introduces the guard.
4. **Navigation targets must exist** before shipping search/onboarding links.
5. **Epic closure stories (like 2.8) are a safety net, not a plan** — gaps should be caught at story completion.
6. **Continue ADR pattern** for architecture vs epic conflicts.
